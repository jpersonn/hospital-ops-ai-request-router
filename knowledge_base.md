# St. Example Hospital — Operations Knowledge Base

This file grounds AI-generated replies to general enquiries. In production this
would be chunked, embedded, and retrieved from a vector database (RAG); at POC
scale the whole KB fits comfortably in the prompt, so we inject it directly.
Same grounding principle, simpler machinery.

## Visiting hours
- General wards: 10:00am – 8:00pm daily.
- Maternity ward: 10:00am – 1:00pm and 4:00pm – 8:00pm. Partners/support people
  of birthing patients may visit anytime.
- ICU: 11:00am – 7:00pm, two visitors at a time, check in at the ICU desk.
- Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

## Parking
- Paid multi-storey car park on site, entry via Hospital Drive. $4/hour,
  $18 daily maximum.
- Discounted weekly passes ($40) available for families of long-stay patients —
  ask at the main reception desk.
- Free 15-minute pick-up/drop-off zone outside the main entrance.
- Accessible parking bays on every level near the lifts.

## Medical records
- Request copies of your own records via the Health Information Service:
  records@stexample.example or the form at main reception.
- Processing takes up to 14 business days. Photo ID is required.
- There is no charge for a patient's first copy of their own records.

## Cafeteria & amenities
- Ground-floor cafeteria open 7:00am – 7:00pm weekdays, 8:00am – 4:00pm weekends.
- A 24-hour vending area is located next to the Emergency Department waiting room.
- Free Wi-Fi: network "StExample-Guest", no password.

## Appointments (outpatient clinics)
- Book, change, or cancel via the Outpatients office: (03) 5550 0000,
  weekdays 8:30am – 5:00pm.
- Bring your referral letter, Medicare card, and any current medication list.
- If you cannot attend, please give at least 24 hours notice.

## Feedback & complaints
- Compliments, feedback, and complaints go to the Patient Liaison Office,
  Level 1, or feedback@stexample.example.
- Formal complaints are acknowledged within 2 business days.
